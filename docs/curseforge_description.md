## ğŸ“– How to Play

1. **Create a New World**: Start a new world with the mod installed
2. **Find the Delivery Area**: A delivery area structure is generated at the spawn point
3. **Grab Your Gear**: Take an Instructions item and bread from the chest
4. **Start the Game**: Press the Game Start block to begin the countdown
5. **Collect Dyes**: Explore the world, gather materials, and craft all 16 dye colors
6. **Deliver to Staff**: Return to the delivery area and right-click the staff while holding a dye
7. **Deliver All 16 Colors**: The first player to deliver all 16 dye colors wins!
