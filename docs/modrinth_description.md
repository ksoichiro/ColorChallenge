## üé® Color Challenge - Competitive Dye Collection Challenge

**A competitive dye collection mod for Minecraft**

Race to collect and deliver all 16 dye colors to the staff NPC ‚Äî the first player to deliver them all wins!

---

## ‚ú® Features

### üì¶ Delivery Area at Spawn
- **Auto-Generated Delivery Area**: A delivery area structure spawns automatically at the world spawn point
- **NPC Staff**: A staff NPC stands ready to accept your dye deliveries
- **Starter Equipment**: Each player receives an Instructions item and bread from a chest

### üìã Instructions Item
- **Delivery Status Screen**: Right-click to view which dye colors you've delivered and which are still needed
- **16-Color Grid**: Visual grid showing all dye colors with delivered/undelivered status

### üé® Dye Collection
Collect and deliver all 16 Minecraft dye colors:
- White, Orange, Magenta, Light Blue, Yellow, Lime, Pink, Gray
- Light Gray, Cyan, Purple, Blue, Brown, Green, Red, Black

Craft dyes from flowers, cacti, cocoa beans, ink sacs, and other materials found throughout the world!

### üèòÔ∏è Village Loot
- **Crafting Materials**: Village chests contain jungle saplings, cacti, and sea pickles to help with dye crafting

### üìä Real-Time HUD
- **Delivery Count**: Track how many dyes you've delivered (X / 16)
- **Play Time**: See your elapsed time in MM:SS format
- **Rankings**: View finished players and their times
- **Delivery Area Marker**: Always know which way to the delivery area

### üéÆ Game Flow
- **Game Start Block**: Press to begin a 5-second countdown, then the race starts
- **Game Reset Block**: Double-click within 5 seconds to reset the game
- **Night Vision**: All players receive permanent night vision
- **Victory Notifications**: Announcements when players deliver all 16 colors

---

## üìñ How to Play

1. **Create a New World**: Start a new world with the mod installed
2. **Find the Delivery Area**: A delivery area structure is generated at the spawn point
3. **Grab Your Gear**: Take an Instructions item and bread from the chest
4. **Start the Game**: Press the Game Start block to begin the countdown
5. **Collect Dyes**: Explore the world, gather materials, and craft all 16 dye colors
6. **Deliver to Staff**: Return to the delivery area and right-click the staff while holding a dye
7. **Deliver All 16 Colors**: The first player to deliver all 16 dye colors wins!
